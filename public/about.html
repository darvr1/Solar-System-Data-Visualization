About

There are few topics that inspire awe, innovation, and imagination quite like the workings of the universe. From science, mathematics, religion, to pop culture, outer space has a tight hold on the human experience. This website, created by Dale, Alexandra, and Yejin of Team DayTa, was inspired by the the wonders of our Solar System. Each of the eight planets of our Solar System have a page with basic facts, which are also represented on the Visualization page. The Visualization graph is a fun way to see the comparisons between each planet and how they differ from one another.
